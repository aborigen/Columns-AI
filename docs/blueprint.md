# **App Name**: Pulp Drop

## Core Features:

- Physics-Driven Merging: Collision detection and weight physics implemented via Matter.js to handle the movement and merging of round objects.
- Precision Drop Controls: Fluid mouse and touch controls for horizontally positioning and releasing fruits into the arena.
- Evolution Cycle System: Logic that automatically upgrades identical items to the next tier in the fruit evolution tree upon contact.
- AI Strategic Lens: A predictive AI tool that reasons about the current heap configuration to highlight optimal drop paths and potential cascades.
- Tactile Progression HUD: Visual feedback system displaying current scores, high scores, and the 'Next Up' preview for upcoming fruits.
- The Danger Zone: Collision boundary at the top of the container with a countdown logic that triggers game-over states upon overflow.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric Fuchsia (#F24472), a vibrant and high-energy hue inspired by the skin of exotic dragonfruits.
- Background color: Deep Blackberry (#1A0C0E), a rich and dark desaturated plum that provides maximum contrast for colorful game elements.
- Accent color: Solar Orange (#F28C44), used for tactical feedback and high-priority interface elements to signify heat and energy.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif that ensures clear legibility in high-speed, chaotic gaming scenarios while maintaining a friendly, avant-garde aesthetic.
- Glossy, high-detail minimalist fruit vectors that transition from simple circular forms to complex organic shapes as players progress.
- Centered vertical focus on the gameplay container with sidebar glassmorphism panels for evolution guides and score tracking.
- Bouncy squish-and-stretch transitions during drops and particle explosions when fruits merge for high tactile satisfaction.
